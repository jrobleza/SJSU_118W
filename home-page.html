About Conference

San Jose McEnery Convention Center March 23 - 27th

Inventing Innovation is a world-renowned conference of technological advancement and human progression. This conference is not only a curation of the best quality innovation content with highly-regarded speakers, it is a one-of-a-kind opportunity to bring students, professionals and people-alike together to discuss emerging technologies.

Mission: To advance the world through the collaboration of diverse minds, dissemination of knowledge and action of the heart.
Vision: To collectively progress the technological pursuits of man-kind

Why Attend

This conference will give you the opportunity to learn from world-renowned innovators in topics regarding the hottest emerging technologies. With an attendance of 30,000 people composed of professionals, researchers and industry shakers, this conference is a phenomenal opportunity to grow your network and exchange dialogue on emerging technology. By attending this conference, you may fulfill a variety of requirements for many information technology certifications and receive tools in making your company more competitive through innovation.
